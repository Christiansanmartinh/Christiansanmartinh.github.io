EU Training Programmes

Context

Training programmes were developed to support public authorities in understanding and applying intellectual property frameworks across operational contexts.

Challenge

Diverse audiences, including customs and police, required content that was both technically accurate and easy to understand in practical environments.

Approach

Designed and structured training content, ensuring clarity, consistency, and adaptability across different institutional contexts and learning formats.

Outcome

Delivered certified programmes that improved knowledge transfer and supported more effective application of IP frameworks across operational teams.
