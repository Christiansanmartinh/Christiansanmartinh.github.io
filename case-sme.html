SME Communication Strategy

Context

Small and medium-sized enterprises often face challenges in understanding and applying intellectual property frameworks. The objective was to improve accessibility and engagement through clear, structured communication.

Challenge

The complexity of institutional language and regulatory concepts made it difficult for SMEs to engage effectively. Communication needed to be simplified without losing accuracy, across multiple languages and audiences.

Approach

Developed a structured communication framework focused on clarity, consistency, and scalability. Content was adapted to different audiences, ensuring accessibility while maintaining alignment with institutional standards.

Outcome

Improved accessibility of intellectual property information for SMEs, supporting better understanding and engagement across regions. Content formats were standardised to allow reuse and scaling across initiatives.
