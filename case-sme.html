Context

Small and medium-sized enterprises often face challenges in understanding and applying intellectual property frameworks across sectors.

Challenge

Institutional language and regulatory complexity made it difficult for SMEs to engage effectively and access relevant information.

Approach

Developed a structured communication framework focused on clarity, consistency and accessibility across multiple languages and audiences.

Outcome

Improved accessibility of IP information, supporting better engagement from SMEs and enabling scalable communication across initiatives.
